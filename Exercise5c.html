Leche Flan is a dessert made-up of eggs and milk with a soft caramel on top. It resembles cr√®me caramel and caramel custard.
This delicious dessert is known throughout the world.
It has been a regular item in the menu of most restaurants because of its taste, ease in preparation and long shelf life.

  1. Ingredients
    • Eggs(12 pcs)
    • Brown Sugar(8tbsp)
    • Evaporated Milk(1/4 cup)
    • Condensed Milk(1/2 cup)
    • Salt(1tsp)
    • Vanilla Extract(3tbsp)
  2. Cooking Instruction

    3. 1.Beat the eggs and seperate egg yolks from egg whites.

    2.Put vanilla extract into the egg, also the evaporated and condensed milk and mix it well.

    3.Heat the pan and put sugar in it until it caramelized. After that, put it in the llanera.

    4.Put egg the mixture of egg yolks in the llanera and let it steam for about 5-7 mins. Then you're done
